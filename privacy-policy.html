How We Use Your Information

Information collected may be used to:

Third-Party Services

Our website may utilize trusted third-party services including scheduling platforms or communication tools that may collect limited information necessary to provide their services.

These services operate under their own privacy policies.

SMS Messaging & Communication

Customers provide their phone number by initiating contact with Code 3 Leads or a business using our system, including by phone call or by submitting a contact or service request form.

Messages sent are strictly related to the customer’s request and are not promotional. Message frequency may vary depending on the interaction and may include follow-up communication regarding services.

Message and data rates may apply. Customers may opt out of receiving SMS messages at any time by replying STOP. For assistance, reply HELP.

We do not share or sell mobile numbers or personal information for marketing purposes.

We take reasonable steps to protect your information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.
