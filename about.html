About Code 3 Leads

I’m not a marketing agency. I come from the same world as the people I help.

I spent 4 years in the military, then over a decade working in HVAC, general contracting, and the oil field.

I know what it’s like to be on job sites, miss calls while working, and lose business because you simply can’t answer the phone.

For the past 10 years, I’ve been a firefighter.

When people need help, they don’t wait. If you don’t answer, they move on.

That’s why I built Code 3 Leads.

A simple system that makes sure you never lose a job just because you were busy doing your work.
